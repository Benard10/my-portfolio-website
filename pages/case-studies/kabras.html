Case Study

Kabras Sugar Cane Field Mapping

20% faster planning support

Context

Kabras Sugar faced challenges maintaining an up-to-date agricultural database. Outdated and incomplete cane field records reduced confidence in farmer profiling, cane supply forecasting, and operational planning.

My Role

I led large-scale geospatial field operations, mapper training, quality control, and structured data integration to produce decision-ready datasets for management teams.

Constraints

The project required consistency across a wide geographic area, fast turnaround for operations, and strict quality requirements for records that directly affected planning and supply estimates.

Approach

The workflow combined field collection, quality control, and centralized geospatial integration:

  • Designed standardized GPS-enabled field collection procedures.
  • Trained and supervised a 70-mapper team to maintain data consistency.
  • Applied validation checks and quality review before database updates.
  • Integrated outputs into GIS datasets and map-ready views for decision-makers.

Impact Metrics

  • 70 mappers coordinated across operations.
  • Improved confidence in cane supply estimates and allocation planning.
  • Faster planning and decision turnaround through cleaner records.
  • Stronger operational visibility for field and management teams.

Skills and Tools

Spatial analysis, GIS data collection, team leadership, and data management.
