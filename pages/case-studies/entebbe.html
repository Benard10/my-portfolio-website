Case Study

Entebbe Airport GIS Digital Twin

Airport operational readiness

Context

Airport GIS datasets required modernization to support safe operations, infrastructure planning, and charting workflows.

My Role

Led data collection integration, 3D mapping, and aerodrome chart-related GIS updates using ArcGIS and AutoCAD.

Constraints

High precision standards, strict consistency requirements, and operational relevance for aviation stakeholders.

Approach

Built an integrated digital representation of airport assets, combining field data, geospatial processing, and chart-ready outputs.

Impact Metrics

  • Improved reliability of operational geospatial records.
  • Supported planning confidence for infrastructure upgrades.
  • Strengthened aviation data readiness and consistency.

Next Improvement

Connect digital twin assets to live maintenance and inspection status for continuous operational intelligence.
